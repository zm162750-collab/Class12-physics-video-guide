💡 Smart Study Tips — Board 2026 ke liye

  • Pehle Oneshot dekho chapter samajhne ke liye, phir Formula Sheet save karo
  • Har chapter ki derivations NCERT se likhke practice karo — board mein step-by-step marks milte hain
  • Quick Revision video exam ke 1-2 din pehle dekho, poora chapter yaad aayega
  • Subjective Q's practice karte waqt answer writing pe dhyan do — presentation bhi marks deti hai
  • High marks chapters: Ray Optics (9), EMI+AC (15), Electrostatics (8) — inhe priority do
  • Electromagnetic Waves (Ch 8) chhota hai — easy 3 marks mil sakte hain, ignore mat karo
